# Theory resources and how they relate to the practical companion

The **authoritative theory-resource list and DOI/landing-page policy** live in `docs/theory_and_package_map.md` inside the unpacked core release.

This companion-level note only explains how those theory resources are used here in practice.

## Practical use of the theory layer

- The **UM8 overview** explains why the companion works with paradigm IDs rather than task-specific algorithm names.
- The **Interaction Matrix** explains why the companion contains edge-family examples, route graphs, and signature seeds.
- The **Compass modules** explain why projection, retrospective extraction, defaults, and candidate route priors all reference dimension IDs and option paths rather than flat knobs.
